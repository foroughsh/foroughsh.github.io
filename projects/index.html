Publications

During my PhD studies, my research focused on self-driving and autonomous networked systems, using machine learning and data-driven approaches to enable intelligent resource management. I worked on designing and evaluating learning-based methods that adapt system behavior under real-world constraints.

A key part of my work involved bridging research and practice, with an emphasis on deployable solutions, reproducibility, and collaboration with industrial research environments. This experience strengthened my ability to translate theoretical ideas into robust, production-aware systems.

  • Machine learning and reinforcement learning for autonomous and self-driving systems
  • Applied research with real-world deployment considerations
  • Data analysis, modeling, and experimentation using Python and ML frameworks
  • Collaboration with industrial research partners and large-scale system environments
